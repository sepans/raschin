"Mimesis is a situation in which the relation between an organism and its environment are blurred and confused." Elizabeth Groz, Architecture from Outside.

In this interactive experiment, I created a mirror that reflects more than one image of each person and show them in different time delays. It was interesting to see how people try to find their real one among their copies.
